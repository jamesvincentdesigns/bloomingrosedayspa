About Me

Alexis is a graduate of Holistic Health Institute of Massage Therapy in San Luis Obispo. She has created a space in which you can get spa like therapeutic massage to soothe the body, mind and soul. She combines intuition and technical skill to provide a touch that allows clients to release and let go of life’s worries. Alexis and her team of talented therapists bring a diverse skill set and have worked within athletic, rehabilitation, spiritual healing and relaxation settings. The Blooming Rose Staff are passionate about helping clients feel physically and emotionally self-aware during the massage and even after you leave the table. Alexis has been a massage therapist for over four years. Her massage style blends muscle-specific trigger point techniques with deep flowing Swedish, as well as several other modalities best suited for the clients needs. Alexis and her staff love working with clients to alleviate pain and bring relief to issues of chronic discomfort, specializing in neck, shoulder and sciatica work. Alexis is committed to sharing her knowledge and resources about the bodies condition. She and her team often recommend stretches as well as corrective exercises to support clients in meeting their goals of well being.
